Please save the images you provided with these exact filenames in the `images/` folder:

## Required Images:

1. **gmail-icon.png** - The Gmail logo (red M with colors)
   - Source: Image 1 from your upload

2. **slack-icon.png** - The Slack logo (colorful hashtag)

3. **google-icon.png** - The Google G logo
   - Source: Image 3 from your upload

4. **notion-icon.png** - The Notion N logo (already exists, but you can replace)

5. **zendesk-icon.png** - The Zendesk Z logo
   - Source: Image 5 from your upload
